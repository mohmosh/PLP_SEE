What is salvation?

Have you ever sat down and asked yourself what salavation is all about?

Well you are at the right place

Salvation in Romans 10:9-10 is basically believing in your heart that Jesus is Lord and confessing with your mouth

This is a gift from God meaning you do not have to work for it; Your works don't qualify you,You just need to believe in Jesus and accept Him as your Lord and Savior.

Some Things to note matters salvation

  • It is a free gift from God, meaning your works don't matter, only what Jesus did on the cross does
  • It is available for every human being
  • You get born again(saved) once and for all.. No backsliding, we can only draw back
  • This is the only ticket to heaven, Jesus is according to John 14:6
